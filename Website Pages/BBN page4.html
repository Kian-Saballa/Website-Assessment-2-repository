Classes are roles a survivor can pick with special abilities to survive the killer.

Customer

"Eat pizza"


Customer is a self-sustaining Survivor who focuses on staying alive, doing Objectives and efficiently getting away from the Killer, with limited to almost zero ability to support others.

The Medic

"Get back on your feet!"


The Medic is a Survivor that is able to heal both themselves and other players that are nearby. It is a support type survivor that vital for helping the other survivors.

The Fighter

"Wait you can fight back?"


The Fighter specializes in the offensive, engaging in direct combat using a fire-axe they carry at all times to stun the Killer. They can inflict multiple debuffs to the Killer depending on the charge-level of their axe. They act as a protector and sentinel, using their abilities to throw Killers off and help themselves and other players escape and stall for time.

The Security Guard

"Stay back!"


The Security Guard is a support-focused Survivor that Slows and otherwise inconveniences the Killer. This Survivor carries a Taser at all times and a Tablet to monitor Security Cameras across the map..

The Technician

"Was that always there?"


The Technician is a Survivor that has the ability to put on a V.A.N.N.I. mask to phase through some obstacles, and place down a Static Field, their Static Mirage, that debuffs the Killer.
