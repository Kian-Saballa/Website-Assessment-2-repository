Things to know

Bite By Night is an asymmetrical survival horror game on Roblox that blends gameplay mechanics from Dead by Daylight with the Five Nights at Freddy’s (FNAF) franchise. Developed by the Bite By Night Dev Team and directed by OzelBlox and BlueRev, the game was officially released on March 28, 2026.

How do you play?

In each round, players are divided into two opposing roles


Survivors: Teams of 6-8 players who must complete objectives (such as repairing generators) and survive until 6 AM to escape. They can choose from various classes like the Customer, Fighter, Medic, Security Guard, and Technician, each with unique abilities to heal, stun, or support the team.


Killer: A single player controlling an animatronic or horror entity (such as Springtrap, The Rotten, or Mimic) who hunts down survivors using special abilities and traps to eliminate them before time runs out.


How are the visuals and graphics?

The game features high-fidelity graphics that differ from standard Roblox visuals and includes a monetized store where players can unlock characters and skins using in-game currency (Scrap) or Robux. While it is an unofficial fangame and not affiliated with Scott Cawthon or the official FNAF series, it has gained significant popularity, attracting nearly 300,000 concurrent players on its release day.
